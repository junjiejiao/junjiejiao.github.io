Junjie's Biography

  • Since November 2020, I have been a Postdoctoral researcher at the Chair of Information-Oriented Control (ITR), Technical University of Munich (TUM), Munich, Germany, working with Professor Sandra Hirche.

  • In October 2020, I obtained my PhD degree in Applied Mathematics (Systems and Control) from University of Groningen, Groningen, The Netherlands, for my thesis entitled ‘‘Distributed Linear Quadratic Control and Filtering: a suboptimality approach’’. My PhD research was supervised by Professor Harry L. Trentelman and Professor M. Kanat Camlibel.

  • I received the M.Eng. degree in Control Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China, in 2016.

  • I received the B.Eng. degree in Automation from Anhui University of Technology, Anhui, China, in 2013.

  • I was born in July 1993, in Lu'an, Anhui, China.

  • My Erdös Number is 4 (Through Harry L. Trentelman -> André C. M. Ran -> Arthur Leonard Rubin -> Paul Erdős). See also here.
